Our Story

.Once upon a time there was two girls named Ashley & Isabella. Both of them have a bad habit of being late & procrastinating. We decided to come up with a solution to solve this bad habit. Agenda Flow, an app that helps you organize, arrange, and structure your daily basis. It sets reminders/ alarms, organizes/plans your schedule, and so much more with subscription.

Our Mission

Our business sets a good experience and provides organization and precision to people who procrastinate. Our goal is to bring out our customers best inner self.This business helps people get their work done and stay organized in a simple, easy way. Its for people who sometimes wait too long to start things or feel messy and confused about what to do first. You show them how to plan, clean up their tasks, and do things step by step. This helps them feel calm and proud of themselves.In the end, our business helps people become their best selves by teaching them good habits and helping them feel more confident every day.
